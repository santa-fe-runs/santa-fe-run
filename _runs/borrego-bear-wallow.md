This extremely popular 4 mile loop runs through beautiful aspen, ponderosa, and fir forest. You will likely see lots of hikers, runners, and some mountain bikers here attracted to the beautiful forest, wildflowers, and riparian segment.

The route, though short, features significant elevation gain and some rough rocky terrain. Best from spring into fall when wildflowers are blooming, but the trail is doable further into shoulder seasons, especially in warm dry winters.

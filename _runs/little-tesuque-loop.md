This little loop is a great snack for a weekday morning or evening for the intense crew, or a great moderate to challenging route for shorter distance runners (or visit flatlanders).

The climb at the beginning will leave any runners lungs burning and their face grinning. The descent to the Winsor is beautiful flow running without too much loose rock, and the climb back to Little Tesuque Creek is steep enough to make you feel alive, but not totally dead. Oh and Little Tesuque Creek is the happiest babbling creek. Expect to see quite a few folks along the creek.

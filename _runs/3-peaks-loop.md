My favorite loop from Aspen Basin. You climb steeply up Raven's Ridge to Deception Peak, crossing the ridge to Lake Peak, then summit Penitente Peak, then descend to Puerto Nambe and return to the trailhead on the Winsor Trail. Some of the most incredible downhill flow running from Penitente to the Nambe Lake trail.

This loop, from the popular Borrego Trailhead adds an elevation, distance, and solitude to the extremely popular Borrego - Bear Wallow loop. The terrain is more technical and steep, but the extra climbing along the Galbaldon Trail makes for some great ridge running.

This route can be made longer by starting at the Chamisa Trailhead.

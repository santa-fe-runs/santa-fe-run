This delicious loop starts at the Norski Trailhead just before you descend to the Ski Resort. This run has a bit of everything: solitude, technical descents down the Winsor to start, smooth flowing downhill forest road running, moderate forest road climbs, and then a steep and rewarding ascent along the gorgeous Rio en Medio before a short, but exhausting return along the Winsor. Take in the sights and sounds of the beautiful riparian areas and meadows along Rio en Medio. One of my favorites.
